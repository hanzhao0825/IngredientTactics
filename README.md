# Ingredient Tactics (料理战术)

A tactical merge game where ingredients are your warriors! Combine ingredients to form powerful dishes and defeat your enemies.

## Features
- **Merge System**: Combine identical level 1 ingredients to reach level 2.
- **Recipe System**: Place compatible ingredients adjacent to each other to unleash powerful skills.
- **Shop & Gold**: Earn gold from battles to buy new ingredients and expand your team.
- **Recipe Book**: View all available combinations in-game.
- **Chinese Localization**: Full game support for Chinese language.

## How to Play
1. Join the battle.
2. Select a unit to move.
3. Use "Wait" to end turn or "Attack/Skill" to use actions.
4. Win battles to earn gold.
5. In the Shop, buy ingredients or reroll for better options.
6. Check the "Recipe Book" (菜谱图鉴) for all combinations!

## Development
Built with Vanilla JS and HTML5 Canvas.
